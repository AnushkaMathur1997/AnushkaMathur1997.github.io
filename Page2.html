About Me

Hi there! My name is Anushka, and I am a self-taught UX/UI Designer. I like to design content that is effortless, delightful, and unabashedly human. 


I've used my skills by doing volunteer work for startups and universities, and have become proficient in creating wireframes, prototyping and doing user research. I'm a teamplayer, and like to collaborate closely with developers and designers to create amazing projects from their initial stages to the end product!


In my spare time I like to illustrate and play video games, and talk about game UI designs, a lot.
