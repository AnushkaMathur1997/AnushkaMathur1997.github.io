Crypto Combat.

The Goal: Design and develop an online platform that allows users to buy and trade game based NFT items.

The Challenge: Designing and developing a cryptocurrency platform with limited funds and time, while working with an international team.



1) Product Definition: The timeframe, features required, success metrics, budget, and the end goal of the product were defined. Jira, Zoom and Discord were the main applications used to facillitate this conversation between the team. 


2) Product Research: To validate the product idea, competitive and SWOT analysis were conducted during this stage. Users from the subreddit r/CryptoCurrency were interviewed to identify the users' wants and needs.The main user problem was that the competitors' websites that they were hard to navigate and lacked information, which lead to user frustration. 


3) Analysis: Using the user interviews, we summarized that our target audience are people between the ages of 22-30 situated in North America. They are looking to invest Cryptocurrency, and due to the pandemic, were interested in apps where they could socialise as well. 

4) Design: Once enough information was gathered, the designs were created using Figma. Crypto website such as Opensea.io, Coinbase and Binance were used as inspiration to ensure familiarity of the interface. Each Iteration went through approval of stakeholders, and 4 users were interviewed before moving from the mid-fidelity design to the MVP.
