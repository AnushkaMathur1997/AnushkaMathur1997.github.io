Crypto Combat.

The Goal: Design and Develop an online platform that allows users to buy and trade NFTs.

The Challenge: Designing and developing a cryptocurrency platform with limited funds, time while working with an international team.



1) Product Definition: The timeframe, features required, success metrics, budget, and the end goal of the product were defined. Jira, Zoom and Discord were the main applications used to facillate this conversation between the team. 


2) Product Research: Gathering and defining the wants and needs of potential users of the product, competitive analysis, SWOT analysis was conducted and presented to the stakeholders at this stage.


3) Analysis: From the research we summarized our target audience are people between the ages of 22-30 situated in North America. They liked to play videogames, and were looking to invest in cryptocurrency.
